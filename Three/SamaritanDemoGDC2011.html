Samaritan Demo

At the game developer conference 2011 Epic presented a brand new Unreal Engine 3 real-time demonstration entitled “Samaritan,” which leverages DirectX 11 support, high-end rendering features, NVIDIA APEX physics technology and other engine upgrades. These new technical additions are available in the March 2011 build of UDK.

The demo content is not planned to be released in public form.

The video can be found here: Video (external link)

Technical details can be found in the GDC2011 presentation: The Technology behind the DirectX 11 Unreal Engine "Samaritan" Demo
