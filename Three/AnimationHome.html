Animation

Animations help to create the illusion of complex movement performed by skeletal meshes. Animations help to make skeletal meshes feel alive or have some sense of personality by defining the way characters walk, the way characters speak and act. From these behaviors, players feel that the virtual avatar is a little bit more real than just graphics on a screen.

Unreal Engine 3 employs a wide array of technology and tools to make it easier for you. From exporting animations from your favorite 3D content creation package, to blending raws animations to create new animations within Unreal Editor.
